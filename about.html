ABOUT

Hello there! I'm Gulalaiy.

I made this website as a part of the Girls Who Code Self Immersion Program. The reason I chose this cause/issue to make a website about is because our wildlife is incredibly important and currently facing numerous threats. From habitat loss to climate change, the challenges are vast, and I believe that raising awareness and taking action can make a significant difference.

I hope that in the near future, notable advancements will be made towards the protection, conservation, and wellbeing of our wildlife.
