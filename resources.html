TO-DOS FOR WILDLIFE CONSERVATION

  • Create a garden with plants native to your area to support local wildlife.
  • Use reusable bags, bottles, and containers to minimize plastic waste
  • Participate in local clean-up activities to help keep natural habitats clean.
  • Offer your time to local conservation groups and wildlife shelters. Educate Others
  • Advocate for laws and policies that protect wildlife and their habitats.
  • Build birdhouses, bat boxes, and insect hotels in your garden.
  • Use energy-efficient appliances and reduce your carbon footprint.
  • Enjoy wildlife from a distance and avoid disturbing their natural behaviors.
  • Follow local recycling guidelines to ensure materials are properly processed.
  • Choose products that are biodegradable and environmentally friendly.
  • When traveling, choose eco-friendly options and respect local wildlife.
  • Share your wildlife observations with local conservation groups.
